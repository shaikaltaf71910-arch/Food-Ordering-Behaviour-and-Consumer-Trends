Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is to build an interactive Tableau dashboard that analyzes food ordering behaviour and consumer trends using historical order data.

The dashboard provides visual insights into:
* Total Orders
* Total Revenue
* Delivery Fee
* Average Customer Rating
* Meal Type Distribution
* Payment Method Preferences
* Order Trends Over Time
* Customer Behaviour Analysis

Interactive filters allow users to explore the data based on different categories and customer segments.

A Tableau Story is included to present the findings step by step, helping users understand key business insights and make informed decisions.

Expected Outcome

The solution enables businesses to:
* Understand customer ordering patterns.
* Improve customer satisfaction.
* Increase operational efficiency.
* Identify opportunities to improve sales and revenue.
* Support strategic business planning through visual analytics.
