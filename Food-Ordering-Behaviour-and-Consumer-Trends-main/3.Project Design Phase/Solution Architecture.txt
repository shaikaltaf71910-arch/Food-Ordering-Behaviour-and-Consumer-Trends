Solution Architecture

Step 1: Data Collection
* Collect food ordering data from the provided dataset.
* Store customer, order, payment, delivery, and rating information.

↓

Step 2: Data Preparation
* Import the dataset into Microsoft Excel and Tableau.
* Clean missing values and remove duplicate records.
* Format fields and prepare data for analysis.

↓

Step 3: Data Analysis
* Calculate KPIs:
  * Total Orders
  * Total Revenue
  * Delivery Fee
  * Average Rating
* Analyze ordering behaviour.
* Analyze payment methods.
* Analyze meal type preferences.

↓

Step 4: Dashboard Development
Create interactive Tableau dashboards using:
* KPI Cards
* Bar Charts
* Pie Charts
* Line Charts
* Filters
* Legends
* Dashboard Containers

↓

Step 5: Story Creation
Develop a Tableau Story that explains:
* Business Overview
* Customer Behaviour
* Revenue Trends
* Meal Preferences
* Key Business Insights

↓

Step 6: Output
The final output is an interactive Tableau Dashboard and Story that enables users to analyze customer behaviour, monitor business performance, and make data-driven decisions.
