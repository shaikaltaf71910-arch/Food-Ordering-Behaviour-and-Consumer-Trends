Solution Requirement

Functional Requirements:
* Import the food ordering dataset into Tableau.
* Clean and prepare the dataset for analysis.
* Generate business KPIs such as Total Orders, Revenue, Delivery Fee, and Average Rating.
* Create interactive charts and visualizations.
* Enable filtering based on meal type, payment method, and customer preferences.
* Build an interactive dashboard.
* Create a Tableau Story explaining business insights.

Non-Functional Requirements:
* User-friendly interface.
* Fast dashboard performance.
* Accurate data visualization.
* Interactive filtering capability.
* Easy maintenance and future updates.
* High reliability and consistency.

Expected Outcome:
The proposed solution provides a visual representation of customer ordering behaviour, helping restaurant owners and business managers identify trends, monitor performance, and make informed business decisions.
