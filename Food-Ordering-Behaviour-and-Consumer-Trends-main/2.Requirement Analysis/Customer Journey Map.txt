Customer Journey Map

Project Name:
Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Dashboard

Stage 1: Discover
The customer opens the food ordering application or website and explores available restaurants, cuisines, and meal categories. Attractive offers, ratings, and recommendations influence the customer's initial interest.

Stage 2: Browse
The customer searches for preferred food items, compares prices, checks ratings and reviews, and explores menu options before making a decision.

Stage 3: Order Placement
The customer selects food items, adds them to the cart, chooses a delivery address, applies discounts if available, and completes the payment using their preferred payment method.

Stage 4: Order Processing
The restaurant receives the order, prepares the food, and hands it over to the delivery partner. The customer tracks the order status in real time.

Stage 5: Delivery
The delivery partner delivers the order to the customer's location within the estimated delivery time. Delivery performance significantly affects customer satisfaction.

Stage 6: Feedback
After receiving the order, the customer provides ratings and reviews based on food quality, delivery experience, and overall satisfaction.

Business Insight
Analyzing each stage of the customer journey helps businesses identify delays, improve customer experience, optimize delivery operations, and increase customer retention.
