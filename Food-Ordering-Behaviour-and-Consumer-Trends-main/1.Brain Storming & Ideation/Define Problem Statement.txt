Title: Problem Statement

Food delivery businesses receive a large amount of customer ordering data every day. However, without proper analysis, it is difficult to understand customer preferences, ordering behavior, revenue trends, and service performance.

The organization needs a visual analytics solution that converts raw data into meaningful insights.

The project aims to develop an interactive Tableau dashboard that allows users to:

• Monitor total orders.
• Analyze total revenue.
• Track average customer ratings.
• Compare meal type preferences.
• Analyze payment methods.
• Understand customer ordering patterns.
• Identify business improvement opportunities.

The solution helps decision-makers improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction through data visualization.
