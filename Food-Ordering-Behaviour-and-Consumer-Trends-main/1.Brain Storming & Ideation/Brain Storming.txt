Title: Brainstorming

Project Name:
Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Dashboard

Objective:
To analyze customer food ordering patterns and identify key business insights using Tableau dashboards and stories.

Brainstorming Ideas:
• Analyze total number of food orders.
• Identify the most popular meal types.
• Compare online and offline payment methods.
• Analyze customer ratings and satisfaction.
• Study order values across different customer segments.
• Identify peak ordering hours and busy days.
• Compare delivery performance across locations.
• Analyze the impact of discounts on customer purchases.
• Identify high-value customers and ordering frequency.
• Create interactive dashboards with filters and KPIs.

Expected Outcome:
The dashboard will help businesses understand customer behavior, improve service quality, increase customer satisfaction, and make data-driven decisions to improve revenue.
