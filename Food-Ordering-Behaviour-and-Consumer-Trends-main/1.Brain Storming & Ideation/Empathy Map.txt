Title: Empathy Map

User:
Restaurant Owners, Business Managers, Marketing Teams

SAYS
• Which meals are ordered the most?
• How satisfied are customers?
• Which payment method is preferred?
• How can we increase sales?

THINKS
• We need better customer insights.
• We should improve delivery performance.
• We need to attract more repeat customers.
• Revenue should continue growing.

DOES
• Monitors daily business performance.
• Reviews customer ratings.
• Tracks order trends.
• Plans marketing campaigns.

FEELS
• Wants accurate business reports.
• Needs quick decision-making.
• Wants to improve customer experience.
• Looks for opportunities to increase profit.

Pain Points
• Large amounts of unorganized data.
• Difficulty identifying customer trends.
• Limited visibility into business performance.

Goals
• Increase customer satisfaction.
• Improve revenue.
• Optimize business operations.
• Make data-driven decisions using Tableau dashboards.
