Dashboard and Story

Dashboard Overview
The **Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Dashboard** is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to provide meaningful insights into customer ordering behaviour, revenue performance, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
The dashboard presents key business metrics and visualizations in a simple and interactive format, enabling users to monitor business performance and identify trends that support strategic decision-making.

Dashboard Objectives
The dashboard aims to:
* Monitor overall business performance.
* Analyze customer ordering behaviour.
* Identify popular meal categories.
* Evaluate revenue generation.
* Measure customer satisfaction.
* Compare payment method preferences.
* Support data-driven business decisions.


Dashboard Components:
KPI Cards
The dashboard includes four key performance indicators (KPIs):

Total Orders
Displays the total number of food orders placed during the selected period. This KPI provides a quick overview of customer demand.

Total Order Value
Shows the total revenue generated from customer orders. It helps evaluate business growth and sales performance.

Delivery Fee
Represents the total delivery charges collected. This KPI helps analyze delivery-related income.

Average Rating
Displays the average customer rating, reflecting customer satisfaction with food quality and delivery service.


Dashboard Visualizations

Meal Type Analysis
**Chart Type:** Bar Chart
This visualization compares different meal categories based on the number of orders. It helps identify the most popular meal types and customer preferences.
**Business Insight:**
Restaurants can focus on highly demanded meal categories to increase customer satisfaction and revenue.


Payment Method Analysis
**Chart Type:** Pie Chart
This chart shows the distribution of customer payment methods, such as Cash, Credit/Debit Card, and Online Payments.

**Business Insight:**
Understanding payment preferences helps businesses improve payment convenience and customer experience.

Order Trend Analysis
**Chart Type:** Line Chart
This visualization illustrates changes in the number of orders over time, allowing businesses to identify peak ordering periods and seasonal trends.

**Business Insight:**
Businesses can use these trends for demand forecasting, inventory planning, and staff scheduling.

Customer Rating Analysis
**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

This chart displays the distribution of customer ratings and helps evaluate overall customer satisfaction.

**Business Insight:**
Lower ratings indicate areas where improvements in food quality or delivery services may be required.


Dashboard Filters
Interactive filters allow users to analyze data dynamically.

Examples include:
* Meal Type
* Payment Method
* Customer Category
* Rating
* Date (if applicable)

These filters enable users to customize the dashboard based on their analytical requirements.


Interactivity Features
The dashboard includes:
* Interactive filters
* Highlight actions
* Tooltips
* Dynamic labels
* Responsive layout
* Dashboard containers for organized presentation

These features improve usability and make the dashboard easier to explore.



Tableau Story
The Tableau Story presents the dashboard insights in a logical sequence, helping users understand the complete business analysis.

Story Point 1 – Business Overview
Introduces the project and presents the overall business performance using KPIs.

Story Point 2 – Customer Ordering Behaviour
Explains customer ordering patterns and highlights the most popular meal categories.

Story Point 3 – Revenue Performance
Analyzes total order value and delivery fee to evaluate business profitability.

Story Point 4 – Payment Preferences
Illustrates customer payment behaviour and identifies the most commonly used payment methods.

Story Point 5 – Customer Satisfaction
Analyzes customer ratings to measure service quality and identify opportunities for improvement.

Story Point 6 – Business Insights
Summarizes the key findings from the dashboard and provides recommendations for improving customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and overall business performance.


Key Findings
* Customer ordering patterns can be clearly identified using interactive visualizations.
* Popular meal categories contribute significantly to total orders.
* Revenue trends help evaluate business performance over time.
* Customer ratings provide valuable feedback for service improvement.
* Payment method analysis supports better customer convenience.
* Interactive filters enable detailed and flexible analysis.


Business Benefits
The dashboard offers several advantages:
* Supports informed decision-making.
* Improves customer satisfaction.
* Identifies sales opportunities.
* Monitors operational performance.
* Simplifies business reporting.
* Enhances understanding of customer behaviour.

Conclusion
The Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Dashboard successfully transforms raw food ordering data into interactive and meaningful visual insights. Through KPI cards, charts, filters, and a Tableau Story, the dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance and customer behaviour. It enables restaurant owners and managers to make informed decisions, improve operational efficiency, and enhance the overall customer experience through data-driven analytics.
